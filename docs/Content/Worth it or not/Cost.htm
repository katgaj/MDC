      Cost

       

       

      Cross-country skiing is cheaper compared to down-hill skiing. There is no need to buy lift tickets like for alpine skiing. All it takes is a pair of poles, skis, and boots.

      When you are a beginner, you preferably should rent the equipment to check if this is the right activity for you.

      If you feel comfortable skiing, in the long run it will probably be more cost-efficient to buy your own skis, boots and poles.

      Check the prices here:
