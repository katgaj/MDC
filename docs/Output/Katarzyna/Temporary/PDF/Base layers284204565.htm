Base layers

 

 

Base layers are close-fitting garments that are often made of wool or polypropylene (fancy plastic). Some folks refer to these as long underwear. They wick moisture away from your skin and help you retain heat. They are lightweight, breathable, and allow for a full range of motion.

Base layers are typically composed of:

  • wind briefsmen's underpants with softshell front panel that keeps you warm in cold and windy conditions. (wind blocking) or underwear,
  • a pair of leggings
  • a long sleeve

NOTE: The base layers don’t have to be the most high-tech. Look for something made of a lightweight material, that breathes well, and is not made of cotton. In exceptionally cold conditions, you may stack 2 base layers with the lighter layer underneath.
