Who is it for?

 

 

Cross-country skiing is accessible to people of different ages and fitness levels, making it a versatile and enjoyable winter activity.

While on the traila route that has been laid out, constructed and maintained specifically for cross-country skiing you will meet children, middle-aged people as well as seniors. Some of the skiers will be just starting to learn, while others will speed up next to you jus to leave you behind in a second.
