Best trails around the world

 

 

Cross-country skiing started in Scandinavia, therefore you should definitely choose this destination to indulge fully in the activity, especially Finland and Norway.

According to www.responsibletravel.com Seefeld region in Austria, Chile and Estonia take the podium places, followed by Black Forest in Germany, Pyrenees in France, the Alps in Switzerland and theDolomites in Italy.

Also, in the United States, regions such as New England and the Midwest offer abundant opportunities for cross-country skiing. The Appalachian Mountains and Great Lakes region feature countless groomed trails trails that are meticulously maintained and prepared by machinery like snow groomers, snowcats, or trail grooming equipment. These machines compact and smooth the surface, creating well-defined tracks or pathsand national parks where you can explore the beauty of North American winters.
