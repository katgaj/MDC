Why bother?

There are many reasons why cross-country skiing has so many fans, some of them being:

 

  • wandering around on the trail among beautiful winter landscapes lets you free your mind and get totally relaxed
  • hard physical effort gives you a massive boost of adrenaline, forget about the bad mood
  • you get fit as your whole body is involved in activity
  • you do not wear those heavy skiing boots
  • it is quite easy to learn
  • you do not need any expensive equipment
  • you may enjoy the activity alone or with your family or a group of friends.
