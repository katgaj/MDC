Work on your core

 

 

For a nordic skier, training your arms is as important as cardio. In fact, cross-country skiers rely on upper body power for up to 50% of their power output. While cardio helps you build endurance, you need your core to be strong as well in order to ski. The power of your propulsion comes from your abs and lower back; therefore, you will need to focus on strength exercises targeting these parts. Therefore, you need your core to be strong in order to ski. Focus on strength exercises targeting your abs and lower back.

 

Try:

  • crunches
  • windshield wipers
  • Russian twists
  • supine leg lifts
  • supermans.
